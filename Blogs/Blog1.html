Introduction to PUBG Mobile Lite

PUBG Mobile Lite is a streamlined version of the popular battle royale game, PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG) Mobile, specially designed for devices with lower RAM and storage. The game retains the core mechanics of PUBG Mobile but features:

  • Smaller Map: 2x2 km for faster gameplay.
  • Reduced Player Count: 60 players per match.
  • Optimized Graphics: Suitable for low-end devices.
  • Quicker Matches: Average of 10 minutes per game.
  • Simplified Mechanics: Intuitive controls for easier play.

These characteristics make PUBG Mobile Lite accessible to a broader audience, offering a competitive yet accessible gaming experience.

The ban on PUBG Mobile Lite in India can be attributed to several factors:
